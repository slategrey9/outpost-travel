Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Outpost Travel takes your privacy seriously. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. We keep it straightforward — because that's how we work.

Outpost Travel is operated by Kristin (Singapore), as an independent affiliate of Alchemist Travel. References to "Outpost Travel", "we", "us", or "our" in this policy refer to this business.

What information we collect

We collect personal information in the following ways:

  • When you contact us — via WhatsApp or email, you may share your name, contact details, travel preferences, and trip details. We collect what you choose to share.
  • When you subscribe to our newsletter — we collect your email address.
  • When we make bookings on your behalf — we may collect additional details (such as passport information or dietary requirements) as needed to complete your reservation. This information is shared only with the relevant hotels or suppliers.

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or website analytics tools that collect personal data.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiry and provide travel planning services
  • To make hotel and travel bookings on your behalf
  • To send you travel inspiration and updates, if you have subscribed to our newsletter
  • To improve the service we offer over time

We will not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Who we share your information with

To deliver our services, we may share relevant details with:

  • Hotels and travel suppliers — only what is required to complete a booking
  • Alchemist Travel — our host agency, for booking fulfilment purposes
  • Email marketing platform — if you have subscribed to our newsletter, your email address is stored with our email service provider

All third parties we work with are required to handle your data responsibly and in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

How long we keep your information

We retain your personal information for as long as is necessary to provide our services and meet any legal obligations. If you ask us to delete your information, we will do so promptly — unless we are required by law to retain it.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Ask us to delete your information
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, simply email us at hello@outposttravel.co and we will respond promptly.

Data security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information. Communications via WhatsApp are end-to-end encrypted. We do not store sensitive information (such as payment details) ourselves.

Applicable law

This policy is governed by the Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA) of Singapore. If you are based in the European Union or United Kingdom, you may have additional rights under the GDPR or UK GDPR. We will honour those rights on request.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy or how your data is handled, please get in touch at hello@outposttravel.co.
