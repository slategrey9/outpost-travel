A Note from Kristin

The effortless way
to go further.

For over a decade, I've worked in corporate finance, M&A and tech across New York and Singapore. In that time, I've had the privilege of working with some of the most brilliant, driven individuals I've ever met.

Finance and tech folk, professionals, founders and entrepreneurs. People who hold themselves to an exceptionally high standard at work and in life, and throw themselves into it with a level of intensity that most will never understand.

After working this long and this hard, sometimes all you want is a holiday to recharge. But planning a genuinely great trip takes real time: weekends lost to competing reviews, tabs that multiply, decisions that feel no clearer at the end than at the start. For people whose time is already at a premium, that's a trade-off that rarely wins.

In the end, you either burn through countless weekends planning an epic trip that leaves you exhausted before you've even packed your bags, or holidays default to whatever was easiest: a last-minute booking, or the same destination as last time.

Sound familiar? I get it, because I've been there.

The idea for Outpost Travel came to me on an ordinary day, when I was still in my old job. The thought was simple: what if someone took care of the details so travel could feel the way it's supposed to? Effortless. Curious. Worth every moment.

In my last role leading M&A at a Singapore tech company, I negotiated deals and paid attention to the details that others missed. That instinct didn't go anywhere when I left; it just found a new home.

Outpost Travel is for the people I used to work alongside. If you hold yourself to a high standard professionally, and want someone to bring that same care to your travel; someone who will genuinely think through every detail so you don't have to: that's exactly what we do.

Someone who knows your preferences, and has the right relationships with the right hotels and operators. The kind of insider who knows which property has the best house reef, which safari camp has the best viewing opportunities, and which operators are worth your time. Where the staff knows your name, and you're well looked after from the moment you arrive.

All you have to do is show up.

If this sounds like you, let's have a chat. I'd love to make your next trip happen.

Kristin Founder, Outpost Travel
