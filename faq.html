How we work

Outpost Travel is a luxury travel concierge based in Singapore. We handle hotel bookings with preferred partner access that unlocks VIP benefits, and end-to-end planning for more complex journeys. Whether it's a single hotel stay or a multi-week expedition, we take care of the details so you can focus on the trip itself.

A discovery call is a short, no-obligation conversation — usually 15 to 20 minutes — where we'll talk through your trip, your travel style, and what you're looking for. It's a chance for us to understand your needs, and for you to get a sense of whether we're the right fit.

No pressure, no commitment.

Pricing

For hotel bookings, there's no fee to you. Outpost Travel earns a commission directly from the hotels. Through our preferred partnerships, we also unlock VIP perks at no additional cost: room upgrades, hotel credits, daily breakfast, early check-in and late checkout.

For end-to-end trip planning, we work with a trusted network of ground suppliers covering accommodation, guides, transport, and experiences. Through these relationships, we access trade and net rates that aren't publicly available. Our margins on these typically start from 15%, while still keeping overall trip pricing competitive.

Budgets vary by destination, seasonality, and travel style.

For hotel bookings, Outpost Travel typically works with properties from USD 300 per room per night onwards. In more affordable destinations such as Southeast Asia, rates can start from around USD 150 per room per night.

For end-to-end trip planning, journeys typically start from USD 300–500 per person per day, excluding flights. Each journey is bespoke, so costs are highly variable depending on destination, group size, and the experiences involved.
