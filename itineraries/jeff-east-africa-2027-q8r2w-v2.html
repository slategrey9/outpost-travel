The Journey

13 days across
East Africa

This itinerary brings together two of the most extraordinary wildlife experiences on the planet: mountain gorilla trekking in the ancient Bwindi Impenetrable Forest, and the Great Migration in the northern Serengeti at peak river-crossing season.

The journey moves from Uganda into Tanzania — from the quiet intimacy of the forest to the vast, open drama of the plains — before closing with two nights on the Zanzibar Coast, where the spice-scented streets of Stone Town and the ancient rhythms of Swahili life offer a perfect final chapter.

Bwindi  ·  Uganda

Asilia Erebero Hills

3 Nights  ·  Full Board

A short early morning flight from Entebbe brings you to Kihihi Airstrip, where an Asilia representative will be waiting to welcome you and transfer you to Erebero Hills, a serene retreat perched at the edge of Uganda's Bwindi Impenetrable Forest.

Perfectly positioned for gorilla trekking and forest walking, Erebero Hills offers easy access to two key departure points into the forest. After a soul-stirring day spent hiking in search of mountain gorillas, return to camp to unwind with a restorative massage or take in sweeping views of the forest canopy from the heated swimming pool.

You'll also have the chance to connect with the Batwa community, the forest's original custodians — exploring their traditional knowledge of native plants, tea cultivation, honey harvesting, and basket weaving, and witnessing how these practices are being revitalised through a dedicated reforestation project.

Northern Serengeti  ·  Tanzania

Olakira Migration Camp

3 Nights  ·  Full Board

The migratory herds begin to mass on the northern plains before river-crossings begin, usually in early July. Days are warm and dry — the best conditions for game viewing. There's plenty of action as herds continue to cross back-and-forth, and Olakira follows them each year, positioning close to the Mara River as the wildebeest build for the crossings.

Ngorongoro  ·  Tanzania

The Highlands

3 Nights  ·  Full Board

A truly distinctive camp set high on the slopes of Olmoti Crater, where sweeping views stretch across ancient highlands and distant volcanic peaks. Your home here features innovative geodesic domed suites, blending contemporary design with natural beauty.

Days at The Highlands are rich with possibility. Venture into the famed Ngorongoro Crater on a thrilling game drive, join a scenic hike to the Olmoti or Empakaai Craters, or immerse yourself in Maasai culture by visiting a local village. After a day of discovery, retreat to the camp's spa and sauna, or unwind beside the fire with your fellow travellers over a pre-dinner drink in the cosy bar. This award-winning camp promises not just a place to stay, but a truly memorable chapter in your safari story.

Stone Town  ·  Zanzibar

Zanzibar Palace Hotel

2 Nights  ·  Bed & Breakfast

Upon arrival at Zanzibar International Airport, a driver will await you. You will be transferred to your hotel in Stone Town, only a short distance away. Once checked in, enjoy a relaxing afternoon soaking up the sounds and aromatic aromas of the city, before heading to one of the many excellent local restaurants to try some delicious Swahili cuisine.

Stone Town is easily explored on foot — wander through the winding streets independently or with a local guide, admiring the grand architecture and famous carved wooden Zanzibari doors. Stroll along the seafront, visit historic sites such as the former slave market or Old Fort, and seek out one of the many rooftop restaurants where you can watch dhow boats sailing into port and listen to the enchanting calls to prayer rising from the mosques below.
